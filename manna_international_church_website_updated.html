🌿 Welcome Message

We warmly welcome you to Manna International Church – a place of hope, healing, and purpose in the heart of Japan.

In a nation where only a few know the name of Jesus, we stand as a light, sharing the unchanging truth of the Gospel with love and compassion.

Whether you're new to faith, seeking answers, or looking for a church family, you are not here by accident. God has led you here for a reason.

Founded by Reverend Dr. Kwasi Agyeman, our mission is to see lives transformed, destinies restored, and the name of Jesus lifted high in Japan and beyond. At Manna, you'll find powerful worship, practical teaching, authentic community, and a heartfelt hunger to grow closer to God every day.

You matter to God. And you matter to us. Come as you are, and encounter the living God. Amen!

📖 About the Church

Manna International Church was officially launched on August 4, 2024, through a vision given to Reverend Dr. Kwasi Agyeman. Our heart is to welcome everyone—no matter their background—and lead them into a deeper relationship with Jesus Christ.

The name “Manna” symbolizes our calling to feed both the spiritually and physically hungry, just as God provided manna to the Israelites in the wilderness. Whether through prayer, worship, teaching, or tangible acts of kindness, we believe in being God’s hands extended.

At Manna, we are not just building a church—we are building people, families, and futures. Our doors are open to all who seek truth, healing, and a place to belong.
